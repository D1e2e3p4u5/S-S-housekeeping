About Us

Surya House Keeping is a trusted provider of high-quality cleaning and manpower services. With years of experience in the industry, we have built a strong reputation for delivering efficient, professional, and affordable cleaning solutions for homes, offices, industries, and commercial spaces.

Our mission is to create a clean, safe, and hygienic environment for our clients by using advanced cleaning techniques and well-trained personnel. We prioritize customer satisfaction and work tirelessly to ensure every space we clean meets the highest standards of hygiene and safety.

Our team consists of dedicated professionals who are trained to handle all types of cleaning tasks and manpower requirements. Whether it's daily housekeeping, deep cleaning, or providing skilled personnel for various industries, we are committed to excellence in every aspect of our service.

Our Cleaning Services

  • Home Cleaning: Comprehensive cleaning for homes, including dusting, mopping, vacuuming, and sanitizing kitchens and bathrooms.
  • Office Cleaning: Ensuring a clean and professional workspace with desk cleaning, floor maintenance, and restroom sanitation.
  • Deep Cleaning: A thorough cleaning service that includes scrubbing walls, ceilings, deep carpet cleaning, and sanitization.
  • Carpet & Sofa Cleaning: Specialized cleaning to remove dirt, stains, and allergens from carpets and sofas using advanced techniques.
  • Industrial Cleaning: Cleaning large-scale facilities, warehouses, and factories, including machinery, floors, and production areas.
